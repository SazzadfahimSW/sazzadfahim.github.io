01 — About

A dual-stack engineer who ships

I'm a software engineer with 3+ years building and deploying enterprise products at ImpleVista BD. My work spans two stacks most people pick just one of — .NET with Angular on one side, React and Node.js on the other — which lets me own a feature from the database schema all the way to the screen.

I've also spent two years on Microsoft Dynamics 365 as an offshore developer for an Australian consultancy, writing plugins, Azure Functions, and Power Platform automations.

Alongside the product work, I co-authored a peer-reviewed paper on CNN-based brain-tumor classification and hold two Microsoft Power Platform certifications.
